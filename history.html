History of Arcadia

History of Arcadia Founding and Early Years (2019 - Present) Arcadia was founded in 2019 as a sovereign micronation under a monarchical system. Its establishment was led by its first High King, Dexter Welling, who set the foundation for the nation's governance, laws, and traditions. Under his rule, Arcadia began developing its governmental structure, ensuring a strong and organized leadership. Government and Expansion As Arcadia grew, it established a structured government with various ministries handling national affairs, diplomacy, military operations, and public services. The nation continued to develop its identity through laws, cultural traditions, and diplomatic relations with other micronations. Present and Future Today, Arcadia remains committed to its principles of leadership, unity, and national prosperity. With a stable government and a clear vision, it continues to grow, fostering a strong sense of identity and community among its citizens. The nation looks toward the future, striving to expand its influence, strengthen its institutions, and uphold its values.
