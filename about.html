About Arcadia

About Arcadia Arcadia is a sovereign micronation founded in 2019 as a monarchy. It was established to uphold the values of governance, order, and national unity. Founding and Leadership Arcadia was founded by its first High King, Dexter Welling, who laid the foundation for the nation’s government and traditions. Under his rule, the monarchy was established as the core of Arcadian governance. The current High King, Mark Blakney, ascended to the throne and played a crucial role in shaping Arcadia’s future. He is the author of the Arcadian Constitution, which defines the nation's laws, government structure, and the rights of its citizens. Government and Structure Arcadia is ruled by the High King, who holds both executive and legislative powers alongside the High Council. The government is designed to ensure stability while granting essential rights and freedoms to its citizens. The High Council and various ministries oversee national affairs, including diplomacy, military operations, and internal governance. Mission and Values Arcadia is dedicated to: Maintaining a strong and stable government. Ensuring the rights and well-being of its citizens. Fostering diplomatic relations with other nations and micronations. Upholding the principles of leadership, order, and national prosperity. Arcadia continues to grow, driven by a commitment to its citizens and a vision of a well-governed, unified nation.
